## 🔭 Current Research & Projects

### 🌞 SolarEye *(M.E. Thesis — Active)*
> **A Deep Learning Based Drone Vision System for Scalable Solar Panel Detection and Mapping in Developing Regions: SolarEye**

A dual-engine AI system combining **YOLOv26 computer vision** (mAP50: 0.532) with **Random Forest thermal regression** (R²: 97.48%, MAE: 0.82°C) to predict heat anomalies and soiling-induced degradation from standard RGB images — eliminating expensive thermal camera dependency. Includes Folium-based heat mapping for O&M scheduling and urban climate policy. Designed for sub-$500 hardware deployment in developing regions.

`PyTorch` `YOLOv26` `Scikit-learn` `Folium` `Open-Meteo API` `Pandas` `OpenCV`
